The Beauty of Flower Tea

Chrysanthemum

This tea is extremely popular in China and other Asian countries. It is made mostly from two varieties of chrysanthemum, Chrysanthemum indicum and Chrysanthemum morifolium. Most Asian grocery stores will sell it dried, or you can order it online. It is touted to have many health benefits. Teas made from the yellow flowers are said to be sweet-smelling and -tasting, while those made from the white flowers are bitter. Chrysanthemum tea is often sweetened with rock sugar. I am lucky to live in New York, where this tea is easy to find.
